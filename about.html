About our school

Beginners’ Basic Schools a centre of academic excellence located in the central area of Aba metropolis presently at 20/22 Uche Street, Eziama, Aba -North Local Government Area. The school took off on Monday 11th September, 2006 with interest in building up leaders with great believe in themselves and work independently in line with established and approved ethics. With her philosophy of upholding high standards of teaching and learning, the school consciously works to ensure that approved best practices are sustained and that the standard never fades. Within the educational zone, the school stands out as one of the few schools that uphold the principle of zero tolerance to cheating and all forms of examination malpractice. This has given her an edge and recognition at different quarters. We believe that every child has inbuilt potentials that needs to be fanned up, as such we treat them equally to bring out the best in them.
We believe that through hard work and serious approach to all our activities, success and excellent performance is sure to come. We see regular attainment to great heights in our pupils because we pay great attention to every detail in our learning process and attach great value to everything we do. Thereby borrowing a leaf from John D. Rockefeller who said that “the secret of success is to do the common things uncommonly well’. This has paid off greatly as seen in our pupils’ excellent performance in all their external examination with credits and distinctions. Our greatest joy is that our pupils achieve these great feats independently, are souring to greatness and will never cease because we are determined to sustain our motto of Unfading Standard

  • Value Statements

Reverence for God: We value and promote reverence for God as Creator and Living Force, in and with us at every moment.
Reverence for Self: Recognizing ourselves as God’ handiwork we are motivated to be dignified, responsible for our own well-being, accepting of ourselves, self-discipline, joyful and good.
Reverence for Others:Beginning from the premise that God created all things good, we recognize the intrinsic value and potential of others and treat them with respect regardless of circumstances. This leads us to be compassionate, hospitable, forgiving and caring.
Reverence for Environment: We recognize that God has given us the wonderful gift of the world and that we are entirely dependent on the environment for every aspect of our existence. We therefore act out of the belief that it is vital for each person to help keep the air clean, the water pure and the land verdant and healthy.
Reverence for Honesty In believing in the value of truth, we seek to promote purity of thought, word and deed in our everyday lives.

  1. Our History
    Beginners Basic Schools, Aba is a comprehensive preschool and primary education setting running a 4-year preschool (pre-nursery to kindergarten) and a six year primary (grade one to grade six).
  2. Our Vision
    To build up a cream of young minds with high moral and intellectual disposition that will make them stand out as peace loving, focused, poised leaders and independent entrepreneurs in our interconnected world.
  3. Our Mission
    To provide quality and first class primary education that will inculcate the ethics of independent achievement necessary to develop the knowledge, skill and attitude needed to become great in all spheres of life through continues participation.
  4. Curriculum
    The academic program is organized and carried out in line of curriculum developed by the Nigerian Edu. Research and Development Council and in pursuance of the 9 year Basic Edu. Programme.
